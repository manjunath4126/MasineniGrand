Who We Are

Hotel Masineni Grand was born from a singular vision — to create a sanctuary of luxury in the heart of Anantapur that rivals the finest hotels in the world, while remaining rooted in the authentic warmth of Indian hospitality.

From the moment you step through our doors, every detail has been curated to ensure your stay transcends the ordinary. Our team of dedicated hospitality professionals is committed to anticipating your every need.

Whether you're here for business, leisure, or a cherished celebration, Hotel Masineni Grand is your home away from home — elevated to its finest form.

Our Journey

Milestones & Heritage

The Beginning

Establishment of Masineni Grand

Hotel Masineni Grand opened its doors in Anantapur with a vision to redefine luxury hospitality in the Rayalaseema region.

Culinary Expansion

Petals & Tasttyy 24 Launch

We opened our two flagship restaurants — Petals for India's finest vegetarian cuisine, and Tasttyy 24 for a 24-hour multi-cuisine journey.

Lifestyle Upgrade

Irish Over Café & Gym

The launch of our charming 24/7 café "Irish Over" and a fully-equipped modern gymnasium elevated the hotel to a complete lifestyle destination.

Recognition

Rated #1 in Rayalaseema

Guests and travel platforms recognized Hotel Masineni Grand as the premier hotel in the entire Rayalaseema region — a title we wear with pride.

Today

Continuing the Legacy

We continue to invest in our team, our spaces, and our guests — elevating every experience to match the evolving expectations of modern luxury.
